Fun with woodworking

Are you creative and like the smell of wood? Then the focus on fine carpentry on the Handicrafts program may be something for you. The orientation should provide knowledge and skills in manufacturing methods and handling of tools and machines as well as knowledge of materials used in the industry. Here you can deepen your knowledge in working with hand tools to create carved patterns, shapes and beautiful details. The training also trains your tool and machine handling, but above all the craftsman in you is trained. You learn the entire craft chain You also develop your skills in furniture and interior carpentry by drawing, designing and manufacturing your own furniture, which will make you aware of form, function and quality requirements. You learn the entire craft chain from idea, design to finished product. In the wood industry today, advanced digital technology is used to handle large wood and industrial machines. In the orientation, you will learn to handle a variety of these machines with functions such as planing, cutting, grinding, drilling, sawing, pressing and much more.
